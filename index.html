WHO
AM I?

I didn't plan to become a creator–marketer. I was supposed to finish engineering, get a safe job, and follow a path that already existed. Instead, I dropped out, packed my bags, and moved to Bengaluru with more questions than answers.

In that chaos, I found The LIT School’s Creator Marketer Program. It wasn’t a classroom; it was a pressure cooker. Instead of textbooks, I was handed real briefs and real brands. That’s where I mastered the art of visual storytelling, building content systems that don’t just look good, but actually drive digital growth. I don't just guess what works—I test every narrative in the wild.

Today, I call myself a creator-marketer who uses AI as a power tool, not a shortcut. I design content and brand experiences that are built to stop the scroll, spark emotion, and actually make people care enough to click, save, or share - not just glance and forget.

If you're a founder or brand that needs content that actually performs - not just looks good - that's exactly where I come in.
